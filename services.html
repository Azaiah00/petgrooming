Service FAQs

Common questions about our grooming services

How long does a typical grooming session take?

+

Most grooming sessions take 2-4 hours depending on your pet's size, coat condition, and the services requested. Small dogs with simple cuts may be ready in 1.5-2 hours, while large breeds or those needing extensive de-matting may take 3-4 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you drop off your pet.

Do I need to make an appointment?

+

Appointments are recommended for full grooming services to ensure we can give your pet our full attention. However, walk-ins are always welcome for nail trims, ear cleaning, and other quick services! You can book online 24/7 or call us at (804) 793-7837.

What if my dog is anxious or nervous about grooming?

+

We specialize in handling anxious pets! Our groomers are trained in gentle, fear-free techniques. We take breaks when needed, use positive reinforcement, and never rush. For severely anxious pets, we recommend starting with shorter sessions to build trust. Let us know about your pet's anxiety when booking so we can prepare.

How often should my dog be groomed?

+

In Richmond's humid climate, we recommend:

  • Long-haired breeds: Every 4-6 weeks
  • Medium-haired breeds: Every 6-8 weeks
  • Short-haired breeds: Every 8-12 weeks
  • Curly/wool coats (Poodles, Doodles): Every 4-6 weeks

Regular brushing at home between grooms helps extend these timeframes.
