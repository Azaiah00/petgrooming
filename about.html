About The Groom Room

Your trusted partner in pet grooming excellence in Richmond, Virginia

Our Story

The Groom Room was founded with a simple mission: to provide Richmond's pets with the highest quality grooming experience using only natural, organic products that are gentle on their sensitive skin.

Located at 5305 W Broad Street in the heart of Richmond, we've grown from a small local salon to one of the most trusted pet grooming destinations in the area. What sets us apart isn't just our skill with the clippers - it's our genuine love for animals and our commitment to treating every pet that walks through our doors as if they were our own.

We believe that grooming should be a positive experience for pets, not a stressful one. That's why we've created a calm, welcoming environment where even the most anxious dogs and cats can feel comfortable.
